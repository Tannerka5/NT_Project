Lifelong Learning and Service

One of the biggest efforts that Christ had during his ministry was his acts of service for others. To accomplish the outcome of lifelong learning and service, I decided that I would look for specifically seek out service opportunities around me.

From small things like doing my roommate's dishes or helping my friends with their homework to bigger things like helping a coworker and her family move to a new apartment, I have been specifically seeking for opportunities to serve others. I have also been trying to be more aware of the needs of those around me. This second part has been a little more difficult as I have always been a little forgetful that everyone has their own trials in their lives that are unknown to me, but exercising that skill, just like exercising a muscle, has been able to make it stronger.

The best part about serving others is that it is a win-win situation. I am able to help others with their problems and doing so also helps to make me feel better and feel closer to Christ. Jesus Christ was the ultimate example of service and I have definitely felt closer to him by better serving my those around me.

Tanner Atkinson - 2023
Brigham Young University - REL A 211
